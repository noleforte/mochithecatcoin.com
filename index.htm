$DUGE is a token on Solana

STEP 1

Go to phantom.app

Download the browser extension or desktop app

Create a new wallet or restore an existing one using your seed phrase

Save the seed phrase securely — it’s the only way to access your funds

STEP 2

You’ll need SOL in your wallet to swap for $DUGE. Here are your options:

Option A: Withdraw SOL from an exchange

Buy SOL on Binance, Bybit, OKX, or other exchanges

Withdraw to your Phantom wallet address

Make sure to select the Solana network when withdrawing

Option B: Bridge from another chain

If you hold assets on other blockchains, you can bridge to Solana using:

Jupiter Aggregator (for cross-chain swaps)
